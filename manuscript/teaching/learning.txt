## Stages of learning

There are several smart ways to learn. They are described in the book [Pragmatic Thinking and Learning](https://learning.oreilly.com/library/view/pragmatic-thinking-and/9781680500196/) by Andy Hunt.

A simplified version is:

0. Awareness: "There is a problem that needs to be solved"
1. Learn the *theory*
2. Get *practical* experience
3. *Explain* it to others

These stages are also a goal when writing this book.
